Hey! Im Marc a designer whos focused on UX design!

My names Marc, and I am a user experience designer. I have always enjoyed designing things. I loved creating experiences ranging from videos games to everyday software, and products.

My Collaboration Skills

Always open-minded to new ideas and feedback, whiel working with my teams. I have very good communication skills, making sure my teams always know what my plan is, what im working on, and timeframes. I also have strong organization skills, making sure I dont get in the way of others work, and keep myself working in an order.

My Design Values

I have a few values around design that are important to me. The first is having a very high focus on improving and maintaining accessibility for all kinds of users. The second is having a strong emphasis on problem solving.

Personal Growth as A UX Designer

I put a great emphasis personally on design values, and overtime, I have built up the skillset of taking in feedback, asking questions how things can be better, and solve problems. I also have the ability to look from a users lens, and make observations on my own of what the users needs are and how to make it a positive experience.
